Dynotron

Find yourself thrown into an alternate universe playing this rhythmic based shooter. You are Dynotron, a powerful and merciless cybernetic dinosaur who has been cruelly awakened from his cryogenic sleep and forced to battle the forces of evil. Fly through space and time to save Dinotopia from the evil meteor Mesozark! It's your job to stop Mesozark before the universe destroyed.

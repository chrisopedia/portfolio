Thin Ice Rescue

Baby penguins are trapped on thin ice. It’s up to you to rescue all of them, but you’ll have to plan carefully – with each step you take, the ice behind you breaks! Meet a variety of penguins, each with their own unique attributes that add new challenges to the puzzles. Can you get all the baby penguins – and yourself – to safety?

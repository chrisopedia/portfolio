About

Happynin Games is a cutting-edge game development studio located in Los Angeles. Formed in late 2009 by Professor Michael Zyda and former executives from Electronic Arts, Activision, and The Walt Disney Company, Happynin develops brand specific games and its own games on the AppStore. Happynin's talented engineers are recent graduates from the University of Southern California; and have already released FowlPlay and Mo' Fishin' this year, resulting in top downloaded categories. Six more games are currently in production and four will be released before the end of the year.

Happynin teamed up with Creative Artists Agency (CAA), the premier talent and entertainment company headquartered in Los Angeles.

  • Happynin Games Merges with Velvet Games

    September 10, 2010

    Happynin Games, Inc. has officially merged with another local developer and its' sister company, Velvet Games. Maker of "Mo' Fishin'", Velvet Games is a small iPhone development company located in Marina del Rey, CA. The merging of the two companies not only doubles the size of Happynin Games employees, but doubles the number of titles in its' library. Combined, the new Happynin Games team will produce and launch 3 more games in 2010 alone. Stay tuned for more details.

  • Fowlplay Lite Released for iPhone

    May 12, 2010

    Los Angeles, Calif. – May 12, 2010 – Happynin Games, Inc. today announced the release of fowlplay lite, available on the Apple® App Store for download. This free, ad-supported version lets players around the world experience the fun and hilarity of fowlplay.

    In the game, players fly as a pigeon and earn points by pooping on the people below, dodging trees, and collecting power ups. Featuring intuitive tilt-and-touch controls and beautiful 3D graphics, fowlplay is sure to delight players of all ages and skill levels. “Fowlplay is a truly unique “third person pigeon” experience,” indicated Michael Zyda, CEO of Happynin Games.

    The full version of fowlplay has additional power-up items, OpenFeint integration to compete with players around the world, new content, and no advertisements. Both fowlplay and fowlplay lite are now available on the App Store worldwide. fowlplay lite is free to download and fowlplay is $1.99. For more information please visit www.happynin.com.

  • Fowlplay Released for iPhone

    March 24, 2010

    Happynin’s first title offers players of all ages and skills a fun time, and can now be enjoyed by iPhone users anywhere.

    Mar 24, 2010 – Happynin Games, Inc. today announced the latest update of its game, fowlplay, is now available on the Apple® App Store for download. Take to the skies as a pigeon flying over a park as players commence the ‘rain of terror’–by pooping on the people below! This funny and addictive game is easy to pick up and play, but will have players coming back for another go to beat the high score. The latest update allows iPhone, iPhone 3G, and iPhone 3GS users to download and enjoy the game anywhere, without the need for a computer or Wi-Fi connection.

    In fowlplay, players earn points and combos by dodging trees and successfully pooping on the targets below. Power-ups can be collected to give the pigeon new powers such as ‘fire poop’ and ‘invicibility’ for a limited time. With OpenFeint integrated, players can easily share their high scores and challenge their friends and families around the world.

    fowlplay is the first title of newly formed startup Happynin Games. “The development team on this has done an outstanding effort and produced one of the best 3D games on the iPhone to date,” said Michael Zyda, CEO of Happynin and Director of the GamePipe Laboratory at the University of Southern California. Featuring simple controls, stylized 3D graphics, and a host of new content planned for updates, fowlplay offers something for everyone to enjoy. The game is now available worldwide on the App Store at http://itunes.com/apps/fowlplay

  • Happynin Games, Inc. Stands Up!

    September 11, 2009

    Happynin Games, an iPhone developer and publisher, began operation on the 11th of September 2009. A team of 14 recent graduates from the USC GamePipe Laboratory has come on-board for this adventure. Our management structure includes senior executives from the Walt Disney Corporation, Amazon.com, eBay, Activision, and Sun Microsystems. We have energy and are rapidly designing the first game we will be building for the iPhone!

Meet the Board

The board is made up of 12 volunteers who dedicate their time and efforts to promoting and organizing the local events put on by this Orange County chapter. These honorable members take pride in knowing they have a hand in shaping the education and events that benefit its professional membership. If you're looking to get involved, these are the positions available. We welcome new faces and new ideas, so please walk up to anyone of us at the next dinner meeting or education event and introduce yourself.

- The Board

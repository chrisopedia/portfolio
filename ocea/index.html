Speakers
Neki Cox, Sr. Stakeholder Liaison, IRS
CPE Credits

Neki Cox is a stakeholder relationship manager with the IRS, and coordinates IRS seminars, meetings and multi-speaker engagements. She has been with the IRS since 1995, has been in the Exam Division as an Auditor, Earned Income Credit Coordinator, and E-file Marketing Specialist. Identity Theft has been the number one consumer complaint reported to the Federal Trade Commission for 11 years in a row. The average victim of incidents related to government benefits spends 141 hours and $2104 to resolve identity theft incidents. The IRS is strongly committed to maintaining public trust in tax administration and protecting security and privacy for taxpayers. Neki will teach us about the Identity Protection Specialized Unit the IRS has created, as well as other available resources. This knowledge is important for us to convey to our clients during the upcoming tax season, and will add even more value to what we do for our clients.

A squeaky hinge lets the entire house know you're opening a door. A sagging door will grate on your floors and your nerves. Sometimes all a door needs is a hardware update to begin swinging like new again. It doesn't take a master carpenter to repair door hinges, just some time, patience, and a little strength (some doors can be very heavy). Although squeaky hinges may be easily fixed with lubrication, larger problems will probably involve replacing the hinges, which is typically a better idea than trying to repair an old set of hinges. So, unless you are attached to your old hardware, plan to replace faulty hinges with new ones.
